    Problem: There are currently very few apps that allow users to search for happy hours at nearby restaurants.

    Solution: Create an app to help users find happy hour deals nearby by adding filters such as times, cuisine, prices, etc. to their search criteria.

    MappyHour was designed to make the process of finding happy hour specials effortless for our users striving to save money. We have observed that existing apps are not meeting the users’ need to browse food and drink deals, which is causing our users to not go out as frequently or to spend over their budget when they do go out.
